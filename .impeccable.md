## Design Context

### Users
Owners/operators (franchisees or small business owners managing 1–3 locations) and managers/shift leads. On mobile, owners want a fast gut-check: did everyone drop and does anything look weird? Managers need quick, on-the-floor actions like approving, verifying, adding a cashier, or checking a drop amount. The job in one sentence: “I just need to know everything’s okay without sitting down at a computer.”

### Brand Personality
Sharp. Honest. Fast. Admin UI is slightly more serious than cashier mode without feeling corporate or enterprise.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark-mode default with optional light mode. Information hierarchy should be clean and confident, similar in clarity to Linear or Stripe Dashboard mobile. Use amber/yellow for warnings and red sparingly for true alerts. Avoid heavy gradients, dated banking visuals, and pure white backgrounds in light mode (use off-white like #f5f5f5).

### Design Principles
1. Optimize for 30-second, one-handed, high-distraction use.
2. Surface answers first; reduce hunt time with clear hierarchy.
3. Make tap targets large and forgiving (48–56px).
4. Ensure contrast and readability on degraded displays.
5. Never rely on color alone to convey status.
